The Mystery of the Missing Jewels

There are 4 unique multiverses that coexist but never interact. Being Dinosaurians, the now more-evolved dinosaur humans, their dinosaur features are greatly dependent from their individual universe’s jewel. Without the jewel, they lose their identity as Dinosaurians, losing all their dinosaur features rapidly from the moment the jewel is gone. When the unexpected happens, the greatest mystery of the Jurassic period occurs: the 4 universes get merged into one – the current SUTD campus. Suspicions and distrust arise between the 4 representatives of their universes (Rubellite, Aquamarine, Citrine, Jadeite) as they have reason to believe that one of them is the kidnapper who just wants their species to be the only one that retains its dinosaur features. How will they find their missing jewels and restore the 4 universes? This is: Multiverse Mystery.
